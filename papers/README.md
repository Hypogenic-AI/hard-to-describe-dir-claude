# Downloaded Papers

## Core Activation Steering

1. **Steering Language Models With Activation Engineering** (Turner et al., 2023)
   - File: `turner2023_activation_engineering.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2308.10248
   - Why relevant: Foundational paper for ActAdd / activation steering

2. **Steering Llama 2 via Contrastive Activation Addition** (Rimsky et al., 2023)
   - File: `rimsky2023_contrastive_activation_addition.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2312.06681
   - Why relevant: Scaled CAA to behavioral steering, key baseline method

3. **Refusal in Language Models Is Mediated by a Single Direction** (Arditi et al., 2024)
   - File: `arditi2024_refusal_single_direction.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2406.11717
   - Why relevant: Demonstrates single-direction mediation of refusal (519 citations)

4. **Improving Activation Steering with Mean-Centring** (Jorgensen et al., 2023)
   - File: `jorgensen2023_mean_centring_steering.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2312.03813
   - Why relevant: Improved method for computing steering vectors

5. **Understanding (Un)Reliability of Steering Vectors** (Braun et al., 2025)
   - File: `braun2025_unreliability_steering_vectors.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2407.12404
   - Why relevant: **Directly shows steering fails for non-coherent directions** — motivates our hypothesis

6. **Angular Steering** (Vu & Nguyen, 2025)
   - File: `vu2025_angular_steering.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2502.04790
   - Why relevant: Alternative geometric approach to activation steering

7. **SAE-Based Steering Vector Refinement** (Wang et al., 2025)
   - File: `wang2025_sae_steering_refinement.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2502.18965
   - Why relevant: Using SAEs to denoise steering vectors from limited data

8. **Understanding Reasoning via Steering Vectors** (Venhoff et al., 2025)
   - File: `venhoff2025_steering_thinking_models.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2502.14829
   - Why relevant: Steering in thinking models, behavior mediated by linear directions

   - File: `lester2021_power_of_scale_prompt_tuning.pdf`
   - arXiv: 2104.08691
   - Why relevant: Foundational soft prompt tuning paper

10. **Prefix-Tuning** (Li & Liang, 2021)
    - File: `li2021_prefix_tuning.pdf`
    - arXiv: 2101.00190
    - Why relevant: Foundational prefix tuning method

11. **Concept Tokens: Learning Behavioral Embeddings** (Sastre & Rosá, 2026)
    - File: `concept_tokens_2026_behavioral_embeddings.pdf`
    - arXiv: 2601.04465

12. **Prompt Compression and Contrastive Conditioning** (Qian et al., 2022)
    - File: `qian2022_prompt_compression_contrastive.pdf`
    - arXiv: 2205.01223
    - Why relevant: Contrastive conditioning for controllability via learned tokens
